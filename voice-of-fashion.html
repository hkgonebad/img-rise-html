The Voice ofFashion

The Voice of Fashion (TVOF) is India’s first daily digital magazine that tracks, defines and leads the most important conversations on fashion, design and culture in India and about Indian fashion and retail globally.

TVOF blends the newsiness of a daily paper, features of a magazine and video and podcast content of a digital portal. It stretches the arc of fashion to intersections it makes with politics, society, art, cinema, literature, graphic and artistic representation and design ideas.

Using the combination of fashion news, views, opinion pieces, reported articles, photo features and shopping lists, both local and global, The Voice of Fashion gives a panoramic cultural view of Indian fashion, its business, politics, design dilemmas, innovations, trends, fashion weeks and consumer response.

Launched in 2018, TVOF is a platform without advertiser plugs and sold spaces which enables a clean and sharp reading experience. It is also India’s only fashion publication with a dedicated section on sustainability with a global-local, socio-scientific perspective.
