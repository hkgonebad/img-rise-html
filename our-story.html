OurStory

RISE Worldwide Limited (formerly known as IMG-Reliance Limited) is a wholly owned subsidiary company of Reliance Industries Limited. The transition to an independent brand identity is aimed at enabling various verticals currently within the sports and lifestyle businesses, an autonomy to operate and create industry driving strategies and world class consumer experiences, as the company focuses on further investing in the segment. RISE Worldwide is India’s largest independent Sports, Lifestyle and Entertainment Company, connecting with over 150 million youth on social media through its various IPs.

Our portfolio includes Sports & Sponsorship Consulting, Fashion & Sustainability Platform Building, Athlete Talent Management, Licensing, Broadcast Production, Lifestyle and Entertainment, with owned or managed key properties such as: Hero Indian Super League, country’s premier football league; Lakme Fashion Week, India’s premier Fashion platform; Tata Open Maharashtra, South Asia’s only ATP World Tour Tennis event; Jio Wonderland; The Voice of Fashion; SU.RE Sustainable Resolution, etc.

A roster of seven cricket athletes managed exclusively by us include Rohit Sharma, Hardik Pandya, Jasprit Bumrah, Shikhar Dhawan, Shreyas Iyer, Krunal Pandya and Suryakumar Yadav under its Sports Talent Management. The Sports Sponsorship & Consultancy advises brands like Dream11, boAt, BKT Tires, etc. in India and globally, and was selected to exclusively market the Indian Cricket Team’s tour of Australia in 2020. The company also boasts of a state of the art Broadcast Production setup that engages in Sports contracts across the world.

With all this and a stronger than ever employees force we are we are here to Rise as one.
