JioWonderland is a fun-filled family experience, unlike anything you have ever seen before. It aims to make wintertime a fun and magical time for all its visitors.

JioWonderland was launched on Christmas Day 2019 by spreading joy and celebration with 4000 under-privileged children through the Reliance Foundation ESA initiative. The premier night had a robust performance by Guru Randhawa, a special appearance by a ziplining Santa and the Hamley’s Grand Parade, premiere night was off to a magical start!

The next 5 days were an escape into Winter Wonderland with exciting attractions, choreographed shows, a unique shopping experience and a carnival parade. JioWonderland wrapped up by paying tribute to the commitment of the Police Force and the Fire Services Department (FSD) of Mumbai with the day dedicated to hosting them and their families.

Jio Wonderland hosted over 8000 members of the police, FSD and their families, and celebrated the season’s festivities with over 100 enthralling experiences.

This one-of-a-kind family event had something for everyone. From the wonder eye-where, you could hop on the Ferris wheel and get a bird’s eye view of the full expanse, to India’s tallest sustainable Christmas Tree which promised to be a visual spectacle.

Playtime was never over at JioWonderland with the Hamley’s booth where your buddies and you could have the time of their lives. The magical carousel sure promised a joy ride and when in need of a break, the shopping market and food court await you. That's not all, because the drone show promised to be a mesmerising experience for your eyes.

Jio Wonderland was attended by the who’s who of the entertainment and sports industry along with their adorable little kids. The guest list included Shah Rukh Khan, Rohit Sharma, Karan Johar, Jhanvi Kapoor, Daboo Ratnani, Shruti Seth, Ranvijay Singha, Tara Sharma, Shreyas Iyer and Ashish Chaudhary to name a few.
